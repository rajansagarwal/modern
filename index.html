Artificially Inspired

Poetry is when an emtion has found its thought and the thought has found words. The vast world of poetry is ready to take that next step.

While playing around with OpenAI and a few neural networks, I came across an idea; immersify the poetry we write. Machines may not have emotions, but they have the ability to intelligently represent our expression on a larger scale.
